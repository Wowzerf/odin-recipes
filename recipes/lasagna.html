Lasagna

Description

Originating from Italy, Lasagna is one of the most famous dishes in the US. This iconic italian dish features wide, flat sheet of pasta that has several layers to it, sauces and other ingredients such as meat and cheese. It makes for a good family meal that is comforting.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
