Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

Buttermilk pancakes, also known as hotcakes, are an American classic breakfast that are usually thin,round shaped,fluffy and the butter adds a pungent flavor. The best breakfast meal that kids will love!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

  2. Mix the wet ingredients
  3. Make the pancakes
